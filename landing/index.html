What is CARE-E?

CARE-E is an innovative emergency medical logistics platform that leverages artificial intelligence to optimize cold-chain delivery routes for time-sensitive medical supplies. Our AI-powered system ensures that vaccines, blood products, organs, and critical medications are transported with precision timing and temperature control.
